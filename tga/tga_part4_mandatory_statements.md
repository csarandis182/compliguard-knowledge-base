# TGA Advertising Code 2021 — Part 4: Mandatory Statements & Required Information

**Source:** Therapeutic Goods (Therapeutic Goods Advertising Code) Instrument 2021  
**Compilation:** No. 1 — 20 December 2022  
**chunk_id:** tga-part4-mandatory-statements

---

## Overview

Part 4 deals with mandatory statements and other required information that must be included in advertisements about therapeutic goods. Requirements differ based on the type of goods (medicines, medical devices, other therapeutic goods) and the type of advertisement.

---

## Section 14 — When Part 4 Does NOT Apply

This Part does not apply to:
- (a) labels of therapeutic goods; or
- (b) consumer medicine information; or
- (c) instructions for use; or
- (d) patient information leaflets; or
- (e) advertisements that comprise only: the name of the goods; a pictorial representation of the goods; the price of the goods; the point of sale of the goods — **and does not refer, expressly or by implication, to a claim relating to therapeutic use.**

## Division 2 — Mandatory Statements for Particular Advertisements

### Section 15 — Pharmacist-Only Goods (Schedule 3 / Appendix H)

An advertisement about therapeutic goods containing a substance in **Schedule 3 and Appendix H** of the current Poisons Standard must contain the following statement, **prominently displayed or communicated:**

> **ASK YOUR PHARMACIST ABOUT THIS PRODUCT**

### Section 16 — Goods Not Available for General Public Purchase

Where: (a) an advertisement is published or disseminated to the general public; and (b) the goods are only available for supply through a health professional — the advertisement must contain the following statement, **prominently displayed or communicated:**

> **THIS PRODUCT IS NOT AVAILABLE FOR PURCHASE BY THE GENERAL PUBLIC**

### Section 17 — Short Form Advertisements

An advertisement that is a short form advertisement must contain the following statement, **prominently displayed or communicated:**

> **ALWAYS FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE**

> **Note:** Short form = radio ad ≤15 seconds OR text-only ad ≤300 characters (excluding social media posts, which are never short form).

## Division 3 — Mandatory Statements for All Other Advertisements

> Note: Division 3 does not apply to advertisements covered by Division 2.

### Section 19 — Advertisements About Medicines

**19(1) — General requirements.** An advertisement about a medicine must contain:
- **(a)** the name of the medicine (within the meaning of TGO 92); and
- **(b)** one or more accepted indications for the medicine; and
- **(c)** the following statement, **prominently displayed or communicated:**

> **ALWAYS READ THE LABEL AND FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE**

**19(2) — Additional requirements for online/remote supply** (where medicine cannot be physically inspected before purchase — includes websites, social media, apps). The advertisement must also include:
- **(c)** the name of the dosage form (TGO 92); and
- **(d)** the quantity of the medicine (TGO 92); and
- **(e)** each active ingredient; and
- **(f)** if one or more health warnings apply — either: (i) a list of the health warnings; or (ii) a direct link to the health warnings.

**Definition — health warning** (s.19(4)): means a warning, contra-indication, precaution or restriction that is: (a) required under a relevant instrument to be included on the label; and (b) reasonably necessary to inform a consumer's purchase decision.

---

### Section 20 — Advertisements About Medical Devices

**20(1) — General requirements.** An advertisement about a medical device must contain:
- **(a)** the trade name of the device; and
- **(b)** an accurate description of the device; and
- **(c)** one or more accepted intended purposes for the device; and
- **(d)** either of the following statements, **prominently displayed or communicated:**

> **ALWAYS FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE**  
> **ALWAYS READ THE LABEL AND FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE**

**20(2) — Additional requirements for online/remote supply.** The advertisement must also include:
- **(c)** each ingredient of the device that is a substance included in a schedule to the current Poisons Standard, where relevant; and
- **(d)** if one or more health warnings apply — either a list of the warnings or a direct link.

---

### Section 21 — Advertisements About Other Therapeutic Goods

**21(1) — General requirements.** An advertisement about other therapeutic goods must contain:
- **(a)** the trade name of the goods; and
- **(b)** an accurate description of the goods; and
- **(c)** one or more accepted indications for the goods; and
- **(d)** where there is a label — **ALWAYS READ THE LABEL AND FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE** (prominently displayed); or
- **(e)** where there is no label — **ALWAYS FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE** (prominently displayed).

**21(2) — Additional requirements for online/remote supply.** Same as 19(2) and 20(2) above — active ingredients schedule substances and health warning links required.

## Quick Reference: Mandatory Statement Requirements

| Goods Type | Channel | Required Statement |
|---|---|---|
| Schedule 3 / Appendix H medicines | Any | ASK YOUR PHARMACIST ABOUT THIS PRODUCT |
| Health professional supply only | General public | THIS PRODUCT IS NOT AVAILABLE FOR PURCHASE BY THE GENERAL PUBLIC |
| Any therapeutic good | Short form ad (non-social) | ALWAYS FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE |
| Medicines | All ads | ALWAYS READ THE LABEL AND FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE |
| Medical devices | All ads | ALWAYS FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE or ALWAYS READ THE LABEL... |
| Other therapeutic goods (with label) | All ads | ALWAYS READ THE LABEL AND FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE |
| Other therapeutic goods (no label) | All ads | ALWAYS FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS FOR USE |
| Medicines / devices / other TG | Online/remote supply (website, social, app) | + dosage form, quantity, active ingredients, health warnings |
